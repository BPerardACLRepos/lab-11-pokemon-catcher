Pokemon Catcher

Instructions

The first step to becoming a PokeMaster is to build your Pokedex. In this game, you'll encounter five groups of okemon. In each group, you can capture one of the three pokemon by clicking on it. As you play, your Pokedex will update your stats for how many times you've encountered and captured each pokemon. Gotta Catch 'Em All!
